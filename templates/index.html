Welcome to WhiteboardAssistant

Overview

Welcome to WhiteboardAssistant, your digital tool for enhancing productivity and creativity during meetings, study sessions, and brainstorming events. Our platform enables users with the ability to draw freely, erase mistakes, and manage notes efficiently.

Drawing

Utilize our intuitive drawing tools to sketch diagrams, jot down ideas, and illustrate concepts on a virtual whiteboard. Adjust brush sizes and colors to make your ideas vivid and clear.

Erasing

Easily correct mistakes and refine your thoughts with our erase function. Wipe off what you no longer need on the whiteboard, making room for fresh ideas.

Managing Notes

Organize your thoughts and keep your ideas intact with our notes management system. Create, edit, and delete notes. Tag and search functionality allows for easy retrieval of information when you need it.
